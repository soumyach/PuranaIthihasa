Ganesha and the Fruit of Wisdom

When Ganesha and Subrahmanya are challenged to circle the world, Ganesha circles his parents, seeing Shiva and Parvati as the living universe.

For children: Ganesha wins through wisdom, not speed. He understands that love can reveal the whole world.
