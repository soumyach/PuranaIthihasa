Vishwamitra's Request

The sage Vishwamitra asks Dasharatha to send Rama to protect his yajna, setting Rama on the path from palace childhood to public duty.

For children: A great sage asks for Rama's help. Rama leaves the palace to learn courage and protect what is sacred.
