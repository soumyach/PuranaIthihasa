# Master Content Coverage Roadmap

## 1. Principle

Every important deity, Itihasa, Purana, festival, and temple should become an experience hub, not a plain article.

Each hub should include:

- quick summary,
- deeper story,
- key characters/entities,
- source trail,
- cinematic retelling,
- kid/family version,
- digital storybook/booklet,
- voiceover/audio,
- quiz,
- drawing/coloring kit,
- talapatra wisdom cards,
- related temples,
- related festivals,
- life lessons,
- community questions/comments,
- share/referral unlock.

The Ramayana page is the pattern. Scale that into a repeatable content system.

## 2. Experience Hub Template

Every hub page should have:

1. Hero
   - title,
   - one-line meaning,
   - visual hero,
   - CTA: `Start Experience`.

2. Quick Summary
   - 60-second version.

3. What You Can Do
   - Read,
   - Listen,
   - View Booklet,
   - Play Quiz,
   - Draw/Color,
   - Explore Temples,
   - Join Challenge.

4. Story / Sacred Context
   - source-aware retelling,
   - variants where needed.

5. Key Names and Relationships
   - aliases,
   - family/companions,
   - symbols,
   - vehicles,
   - associated places.

6. Digital Booklet
   - premium hardbound reader.

7. Drawing Kit
   - line art,
   - trace/decorate modes.

8. Quiz and Games
   - beginner/intermediate/advanced,
   - family room later.

9. Temples and Festivals
   - top temples,
   - major festival links,
   - rituals.

10. Community
   - questions,
   - comments,
   - submissions,
   - points.
