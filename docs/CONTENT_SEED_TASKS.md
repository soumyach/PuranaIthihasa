# Khatakshetra Content Seeding Tasks

This is the operating checklist for turning the site from a polished shell into a credible story, play, drawing, booklet, deity, festival, and temple library.

## Content Unit Definition

Every story, deity, festival, temple, or collection should eventually have:

- Quick answer for AEO
- Adult cinematic narrative
- Kids 5-8 version
- Kids 9-12 version
- Character-dialogue or skit version
- Descriptive SEO article
- Source and variant notes in public-friendly language
- Related people, places, texts, festivals, temples, and activities
- Quiz with solo and family-room mode
- Printable/digital coloring or drawing page
- Premium booklet/PDF layout
- Image set: hero, character reference, booklet art, coloring line art, social card
- Signup/unlock path: email, challenge, referral, or kit interest

## Image Generation Checklist

For each major topic, generate or curate:

- Premium hero image
- Character sheet
- Story scene art
- Text-free coloring line art
- Booklet cover
- Talapatra/sloka strip visual
- Social share card

Image style rules:

- Match Khatakshetra premium temple-archive style.
- Coloring pages must be clean black-line art with large colorable areas.
- Do not use rough symbolic diagrams as finished coloring pages.
- Keep deity iconography respectful and recognizable.
- Generate variants for kids, adult storybook, and premium kit visuals separately.

## Relationship Model

Minimum relations for every content item:

- `appears_in`
- `avatar_of`
- `consort_of`
- `child_of`
- `parent_of`
- `teacher_of`
- `devotee_of`
- `associated_with`
- `born_in`
- `temple_of`
- `festival_of`
- `teaches`
- `source_text`

Use public labels like “Related people, places, texts, and ideas” instead of backend terminology.

## Translation Layer

English first. Then contextual versions:

- Telugu
- Hindi

Do not do literal sentence translation. Produce culturally fluent versions with names, idioms, and devotional phrasing checked separately.
