# Homepage Conversion Brief

## 1. Success Metric

Homepage success is not "people browsed." Homepage success is:

1. Email captured.
2. Account/signup created.
3. User unlocks something.
4. User shares/refers.
5. User joins a competition or kit waitlist.

Primary KPI:

- visitor -> email unlock conversion.

Secondary KPIs:

- visitor -> signup,
- signup -> referral/share,
- visitor -> quiz start,
- visitor -> drawing kit start,
- visitor -> kit waitlist.

## 2. Conversion Philosophy

Use the value equation:

```text
Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice)
```

For Khatakshetra:

- Dream outcome: "I can understand Hindu stories, temples, rituals, life lessons, and history in a way that is beautiful, useful, fun, and shareable for my family/community."
- Perceived likelihood: show premium visuals, free sample, source-aware trust, real experiences, points, community contributions, and clear progression.
- Time delay: give an instant story/quiz/drawing kit in the first click.
- Effort/sacrifice: no forced signup before value, no confusing navigation, no long explanation.

## 3. Above-the-Fold Job

The hero must answer in 3 seconds:

1. What is this?
2. Is it for me/my child/family/community?
3. What can I do right now?
4. Why should I trust it?

Hero should make the visitor read the next line, then the next, then click.

## 5. Recommended Hero

Use Option C for conversion.

Why:

- high dream outcome,
- immediate time-to-value,
- clear free offer,
- low friction,
- naturally leads to email capture.

Hero structure:

1. Small eyebrow: `Stories. Temples. Booklets. Games. Community.`
2. H1: `Unlock your first Khatakshetra story experience in 60 seconds.`
3. Subhead: `Get a cinematic story, digital booklet preview, quiz, drawing kit, and talapatra wisdom card — then unlock more through points, challenges, and referrals.`
4. Primary CTA: `Unlock Free Story Kit`
5. Secondary CTA: `Explore Stories`
6. Micro-trust: `Source-aware. Beautifully designed. Built for seekers, families, and communities.`

## 6. Hero Visual Concept

One immersive full-bleed animated experience carousel that shows the website value, not only one story:

1. Cinematic story opens with epic art and source badge.
2. Premium hardbound digital booklet opens with page-turn motion.
3. Talapatra wisdom strip slides in with audio/pronunciation cue.
4. Quiz cards flip and score points.
5. Drawing kit fills color into a deity/story scene.
6. Temple guide reveals timings, story, route, and local tips.
7. Competition/community board shows submissions, points, and leaderboard.
8. Festival kit waitlist appears as the final conversion frame.

Final focal point: `Unlock Free Story Kit`.

This should feel like MotionSites-style premium motion, but with Khatakshetra’s devotional archive identity.

No split-layout generic hero. No bland gradient. No card-heavy SaaS section above fold.

## 7. First Click Flow

Primary CTA: `Unlock Free Story Kit`

Flow:

1. Modal or first section opens instantly.
2. User selects:
   - path: Stories / Temples / Quizzes / Drawing / Booklets / Competitions,
   - audience: adult / family / child age band / teacher-community.
3. Show preview immediately.
4. Ask email to unlock full pack.
5. After email:
   - open drawing kit,
   - create lightweight profile,
   - show referral/share unlock.

Do not ask for too much information upfront.

## 8. Near-Zero Discovery Friction

Add a prominent "I want to..." selector:

- Learn a story.
- Find a temple guide.
- Open a booklet.
- Get a drawing kit.
- Play a quiz.
- Join a festival challenge.
- Earn points/contribute.
- Buy/join kit waitlist.

Each option routes directly to an experience.

This is better than making users understand the whole site taxonomy.

## 9. Homepage Sequence

1. Hero + free unlock.
2. "I want to..." selector.
3. Featured free story kit.
4. Digital booklets preview.
5. Drawing/coloring kit preview.
6. Quiz/challenge preview.
7. Temple guide preview.
8. Khatakshetra physical kit waitlist.
9. Community/referral unlock.
10. Final CTA.

## 10. Email Capture Offers

Primary:

- Free Ramayana Starter Kit.

Includes:

- one cinematic story,
- three coloring pages,
- one quiz,
- one digital talapatra card,
- one life-lesson/reflection prompt.

Secondary:

- Free Ganesh Chaturthi digital booklet.
- Temple guide updates for your city.
- Join the festival challenge.
- Unlock the Shani Dev audio story.

## 11. Share / Referral Loop

After unlock:

- "Share with 2 families to unlock 5 more coloring pages."
- "Invite a friend to unlock the premium booklet."
- "Submit a drawing, answer, temple tip, or story question to enter the festival challenge."
- "Share your quiz score."

The referral should feel like giving another parent something useful, not begging for shares.

## 12. Animation Requirements

Motion must:

- clarify the offer,
- guide the eye to CTA,
- feel premium,
- load fast,
- work on mobile.

Use:

- initial book opening animation,
- automatic experience carousel,
- talapatra slide,
- quiz card flip,
- coloring fill reveal,
- temple map route reveal,
- competition leaderboard rise,
- points/unlock glow,
- subtle parallax on sacred art.

Avoid:

- endless background loops that hide the copy,
- heavy video that slows mobile,
- motion without conversion purpose.

## 13. Trust Builders

Show quickly:

- source-aware stories,
- age-friendly versions,
- life-lesson and reflection prompts,
- moderated community,
- temple info freshness,
- no child account required.

## 14. First Implementation Version

Build a static but polished homepage first:

- CSS/JS animations,
- image assets from existing library,
- email capture wired to `starter_story_kit`,
- free kit preview modal,
- "I want to..." selector,
- auto-rotating experience hero,
- hardbound booklet teaser,
- quiz teaser,
- temple guide teaser,
- kit waitlist teaser.

Then add real generated pages behind it.
