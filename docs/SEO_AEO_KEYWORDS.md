# SEO + AEO Keyword Map for Khatakshetra.com

## 1. Positioning

Khatakshetra should rank for the intersection of:

- Hindu stories,
- Hindu gods and goddesses,
- temple travel guides,
- festival activities for kids,
- digital coloring/drawing books,
- mythology quizzes,
- premium Hindu culture kits,
- source-aware answers.

Do not chase only head terms like `ramayana` or `mahabharata` first. Win long-tail, high-intent, family/search-answer queries.

## 4. AEO Page Formula

Every page should answer:

- What is it?
- Why does it matter?
- What is the story?
- What is the source?
- What should kids remember?
- What can families do?
- What related story should I read next?

For temple pages:

- What is this temple famous for?
- What are the timings?
- What is the story behind it?
- How to reach?
- What rituals/festivals matter?
- What should visitors not miss?
- What do locals recommend?
