# Digital Experience Spec

## 1. Shared Visual Language

The current site style should become the platform design system:

- Fonts:
  - `Cinzel` for epic display headings.
  - `Cinzel Decorative` for sacred/brand ornament text.
  - `Playfair Display` for elegant section headings and quotes.
  - `Crimson Text` for long-form story reading.
- Palette:
  - deep near-black brown,
  - antique gold,
  - saffron,
  - warm cream,
  - maroon,
  - forest green,
  - indigo/teal accents.
- Layout:
  - immersive story sections,
  - premium archival cards only where needed,
  - gold line dividers,
  - no generic SaaS cards,
  - no cartoonish colors.

## 2. Normal Story Page

Purpose: searchable, answerable, trustworthy.

Sections:

1. Hero
   - title,
   - one-line hook,
   - source badge,
   - primary art.

2. Direct Answer
   - short AEO answer.

3. Story
   - cinematic retelling.

4. Source Trail
   - source text,
   - section,
   - link,
   - variant note.

5. Knowledge Map
   - characters,
   - places,
   - texts,
   - festivals,
   - related stories.

6. For Children
   - 5-8 version,
   - 9-12 version.

7. Play
   - quiz,
   - coloring/drawing,
   - sequence game.

8. Preserve
   - booklet,
   - audio,
   - talapatra card.

9. Continue
   - related story carousel.

## 3. Premium Hardbound Digital Booklet

This should feel like opening a premium hardbound mythological book.

### Visual Treatment

- hardbound cover with cloth/leather texture,
- gold-foil-style border,
- debossed deity/object motif,
- page spread view on desktop,
- single page on mobile,
- warm ivory paper,
- subtle page shadows,
- page-turn animation,
- chapter title pages,
- large cinematic art plates.

### Booklet Structure

1. Cover
2. Blessing page
3. Source note
4. Chapter 1
5. Full-page art
6. Talapatra wisdom insert
7. Chapter 2
8. Parent reflection
9. Child activity
10. Quiz unlock
11. Certificate/share page

### Unlocks

- first booklet free,
- email unlock for premium chapters,
- referral unlock for printable PDF,
- kit purchase unlocks full festival bundle.

## 4. Talapatra Grand Cards

Digital talapatra should be a premium ritual object, not a basic quote card.

Card data:

- Sanskrit/Devanagari,
- transliteration,
- meaning,
- audio pronunciation,
- associated deity,
- associated story,
- festival use,
- printable version.

Interaction:

- swipe horizontal strips,
- play pronunciation,
- save to family collection,
- unlock physical kit version.

## 5. Digital Drawing Kit

### Modes

- Coloring Mode: fill line-art areas.
- Trace Mode: ghost outline for drawing practice.
- Decorate Mode: add motifs, stickers, borders.
- Scene Builder: place characters and objects into a story scene.
- Free Draw: blank themed paper.

### Content Types

- deity portraits,
- festival objects,
- story scenes,
- temple architecture,
- maps,
- mandala borders.

### Storage

Save:

- user id,
- source page,
- chosen colors,
- final image URL,
- submission status,
- competition id,
- parent consent.

## 6. Story Audio

Audio player should support:

- chapter markers,
- transcript highlight,
- pronunciation popovers,
- background ambience setting,
- low-data mode,
- save progress.

Voiceover fields:

- narrator voice,
- pace,
- mood,
- SSML,
- music brief,
- sound effect notes,
- glossary.

## 7. Community and Competition

Competition page:

- festival theme,
- age bands,
- submission type,
- deadline,
- parent consent,
- public/private toggle,
- certificate,
- referral prompt.

User value:

- unlock drawing packs,

Business value:
