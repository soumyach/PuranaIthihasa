Ramanathaswamy Temple

Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu

Rameswaram connects Shiva worship with Rama's journey, pilgrimage, water rituals, and gratitude before crossing the sea.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Shiva
  • City: Rameswaram
  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • How to reach: Rail and road connect Rameswaram; nearest major airport access is via Madurai.

What Not to Miss

  • Temple corridors
  • Sacred wells
  • Dhanushkodi trip

Food Nearby
