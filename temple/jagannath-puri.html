Jagannath Temple

Puri, Odisha

Jagannath is worshipped with Balabhadra and Subhadra, and Rath Yatra celebrates the divine journey toward devotees.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Jagannath / Krishna
  • City: Puri
  • State: Odisha
  • How to reach: Fly to Bhubaneswar, rail or road to Puri, then local transport to the temple area.

What Not to Miss

  • Mahaprasad
  • Rath Yatra context
  • Puri beach sunrise

Food Nearby

  • Mahaprasad at Ananda Bazaar
  • Odia vegetarian food
