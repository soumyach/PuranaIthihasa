Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Kashi Vishwanath is remembered as a jyotirlinga and a sacred center of Shiva's presence in the city of light.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Shiva
  • City: Varanasi
  • State: Uttar Pradesh
  • How to reach: Fly or rail to Varanasi; local movement depends on ghat-side access and security rules.

What Not to Miss

  • Ganga darshan
  • Evening aarti nearby
  • Old city walk

Food Nearby

  • Kachori sabzi
  • Banarasi sweets
  • Lassi
