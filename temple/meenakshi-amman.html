Meenakshi Amman Temple

Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Meenakshi is worshipped as the queen-goddess of Madurai, with the temple celebrating Devi's sovereignty and Shiva as Sundareswarar.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Meenakshi / Parvati
  • City: Madurai
  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • How to reach: Fly or rail to Madurai; the temple sits in the old city with walkable market streets around it.
