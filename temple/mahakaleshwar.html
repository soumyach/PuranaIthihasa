Mahakaleshwar Temple

Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh

Mahakaleshwar is a jyotirlinga associated with time, protection, and Shiva's presence as Mahakala.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Shiva
  • City: Ujjain
  • State: Madhya Pradesh
  • How to reach: Rail and road to Ujjain; Indore is a major nearby airport.

What Not to Miss

  • Bhasma aarti booking rules
  • Mahakal corridor
  • Shipra river

Food Nearby

  • Poha-jalebi
  • Ujjain snacks
  • Vegetarian thali
