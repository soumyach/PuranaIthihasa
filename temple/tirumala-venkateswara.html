Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh

Venkateswara is worshipped as Vishnu who blesses devotees through patience, surrender, and darshan.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Venkateswara / Vishnu
  • City: Tirupati
  • State: Andhra Pradesh
  • How to reach: Fly or rail to Tirupati, then use road transport to Tirumala; official transport and darshan rules should be checked before travel.

What Not to Miss

  • Suprabhatam tradition
  • Laddu prasadam
  • Hill route atmosphere

Food Nearby

  • Temple prasadam
  • Tirupati vegetarian meals
