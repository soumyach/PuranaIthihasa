Siddhivinayak Temple

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Siddhivinayak is one of Mumbai's most beloved Ganesha temples, visited for beginnings, vows, and gratitude.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Ganesha
  • City: Mumbai
  • State: Maharashtra
  • How to reach: Use Mumbai local transport or taxi to Prabhadevi; check special day crowd rules.
