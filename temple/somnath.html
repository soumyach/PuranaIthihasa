Somnath Temple

Somnath, Gujarat

Somnath is revered as a jyotirlinga and a symbol of devotion restored again and again.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Shiva
  • City: Somnath
  • State: Gujarat
  • How to reach: Rail and road connect Somnath; Diu and Rajkot are common air gateways depending on route.

What Not to Miss

  • Sea-facing temple view
  • Evening light and sound show
  • Prabhas Patan context

Food Nearby

  • Gujarati thali
  • Temple-town vegetarian meals
