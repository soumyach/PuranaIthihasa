Kamakhya Temple

Guwahati, Assam

Kamakhya is a major Shakti Peetha associated with Devi, fertility, sacred power, and Tantric traditions.

Key Facts

  • Deity: Kamakhya / Devi
  • City: Guwahati
  • State: Assam
  • How to reach: Fly or rail to Guwahati, then road transport to Nilachal Hill.

What Not to Miss

  • Nilachal Hill view
  • Ambubachi context
  • Shakti Peetha story
