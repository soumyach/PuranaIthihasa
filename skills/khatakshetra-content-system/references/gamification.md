# Gamification

## Progress Model

Use four progress tracks:

- Story Mastery: reading, quiz accuracy, booklet completion.
- Creative Practice: coloring, drawing, crafts, submissions.
- Temple Seva: verified temple tips, useful comments, travel updates.
- Community: family rooms, referrals, local challenges.

## Level Unlocks

- Level 1: Starter story + quiz.
- Level 2: First coloring page.
- Level 3: PDF booklet.
- Level 4: Family-room challenge.
- Level 5: Festival pack preview.
- Level 6+: local leaderboards, contributor badges, event eligibility.

## Better Than Points Alone

- Hyper-local competition: city, family, temple group, school, age band.
- Variable reward: reveal a booklet page, talapatra card, or coloring sheet.
- Completion drive: rings for deity, festival, temple, Itihasa paths.
- Competence feedback: story mastery score, quiz level, contribution quality.

## Account Association

Until production auth is live, store progress locally with an anonymous ID. In production, map anonymous progress to Supabase `app_users` after email or login.
