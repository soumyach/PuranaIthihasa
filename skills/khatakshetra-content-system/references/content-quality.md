# Content Quality

## Public Copy Bans

Do not show: seed, placeholder, TODO, planned, validation, content graph, KG, nodes, edges, source review, pipeline, prototype, production version, internal, needs exact source validation.

Use instead:

- “Story path”
- “Related paths”
- “Traditional retelling”
- “Variant traditions”
- “Coming next”
- “Unlock”
- “Booklet”
- “Family challenge”

## Story Modes

Adult cinematic:

- Open with a vivid tension or question.
- Keep structure clear: situation, conflict, choice, consequence, meaning.
- Avoid gore or sensationalism for family pages.

Kids 5-8:

- Short paragraphs.
- One emotion per paragraph.
- Clear moral without preaching.

Kids 9-12:

- Add nuance, motive, and consequence.
- Include one discussion question.

Dialogue/skit:

- 2-5 characters.
- Short stage directions.
- Performable in 3-6 minutes.

Descriptive SEO:

- Define the topic in the first 40 words.
- Include source, meaning, related festivals, activities, and FAQ.

## Source Notes

Use exact source names when known. When not known, say “traditional retelling” and explain variants gently. Do not imply scholarly certainty without a source trail.
